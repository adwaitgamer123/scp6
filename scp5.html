Codingal is the most loved online coding school for kids & teens, with a mission to inspire school students to fall in love with coding at a young age. Launched in September 2020

Codingal has already empowered 500,000+ students by motivating them to start learning coding via competitions and high-quality coding education. All classes are taught live by vetted and trained teachers who are experts at programming and only from a Computer Science background. The coding curriculum is based on a standardized K-12 Computer Science Framework, which students find very fun & engaging.
